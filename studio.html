06 — The Curated Craft

Handmade.
Intentional.
Irreplaceable.

We don't believe in moving fast. The Studio exists because we got tired of buying things that meant nothing — and because we kept meeting makers whose work deserved a wider audience. Every object here is chosen slowly, carried carefully, and sold honestly. No trends. No overstock. When something is gone, it's gone.
